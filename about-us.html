About Mina Kumari

Shakti Entertainment is the living dream of the Smt. Meena Kumari. She is the Daughter of the Great Sitar vadak Late.Shri Govind Chaudhary. Music runs in the blood of the Smt.Meena Kumari and with her father's inspiration and vision to create a platform to all the needy singers and musical fraternity she took a bold step to start the shakti entertainment. Though belonging to the small village in north and mere housewife but obstinately unmoving talent of singing made her achieve the dream she believed. She is also known for her philanthropist works towards stray dogs and other animals in Navi Mumbai and Panvel locality which she is lone handling from past decade.

Discover Meena Kumari's Love for Embossing Painting & Dogs

Late Shri Govind Chaudhary My Father was born in Paliganj Village in Bihar. Father did his Engineering Degree from capital of Bihar Patna and worked in PWD department. Famous Gandhi Setu was executed under his supervision in Patna. At that tiem he was working on the post of Superintendent Engineer. My father loved music, he liked playing Sitar more. He studied Sitar at the institute of Music Patna University completed Diploma in Sitar in 1951 under the guidance of his teacher Shri L N Kapoor. He played Sitar from the year 1960 to 1970. He used to play Sitar at Akashwani Radio Station at Patna. During Durga Pooja the veteran Artists of that Era staged programs of artist life like Hema Malini and Sitara Devi dance. He used to play Sitar in programs. Father used to play Sitar well and also played Tabla well. He loved Raag Hamir, Walmunsh, Yaman, Malhar, Bhairav, Tilak Kamod and used to play in Sitar. He used to host shows . He died on 31st May 1981. He was a hard working person and used to help the poor and the needy. Till his last breath he did good word and deeds for the poor.
