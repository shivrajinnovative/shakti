Dil ne phir yaad kiya

November 24, 2024 @ 8:30 PM

Dil ne phir yaad kiya 2024 is a celebration of an unforgettable night of fun and unlimited music from the very own voice and beat from the talented performers of Drishti Foundation presented by Shakti Entertainment as a tribute to the legends of Indian music. This year’s addition includes an golden era of songs from 50s,60s,70s,80s and 90s performed by the talented and well known blind performers of DRISHTI Foundation as a tribute to legends. Drishti Foundation is well known organization whose singers and musicians have performed all over the world from the past 10+ years. Shakti Entertainment is very lucky to bring this bit of less fortunate people, but Artists full of talent to NAVI MUMBAI for the 1st Time to witness their extra ordinary performance live on 24th NOV. 2024 from 8.30PM onwards
