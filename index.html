About Shakti Entertainment

Shakti Entertainment is a musical group where our talented singers from all over India sings different genres of songs focusing mostly on old songs. Singers perform vocal music in variety of styles. Some specialize in a particular vocal style, such as opera or jazz; others perform in variety of musical genres. Singers, particularly those who specialize in opera or classical music, may perform in different languages, such as French or Italian. Opera and musical theater singers act out a story by singing instead of speaking the dialogue. Some singers become background singers, providing vocals to harmonize with or support a lead singer. In some cases, musicians and singers write their own music to record and perform Some musicians and singers give private music lessons to children and adults. Others with a background in music may teach music in public and private schools, but they typically need a bachelor’s degree and a teaching license. We have a mix group of talent of young and the old singers who sing the golden days songs of 50s, 60s, 70s, 80s and the 90s.. Some of the famous singers whose songs will be sung by our talented singers as the great Hemant Kumar, Lata Mangeshkar, Talat Mahmood, Mohd. Rafi, Kishor Kumar, R.D. Burman, S.D.
